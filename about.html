My full story

I am proud to call myself an engineer. It is a fundamental part of my identity, and it has enhanced every aspect of my life in some way. For example, I manufactured my wife's engagement ring out of titanium round stock, using Solidworks for design and a Wire EDM for cutting. I have also won toy-inventing competitions, coded games using Python, and added automation to our home. I am never afraid to try something new, even if I have no prior knowledge, because I know I will learn a great deal along the way.

Applying to Hack-Reactor was a natural progression in my journey as an engineer. I was drawn to software engineering because of the virtually limitless possibilities for projects, with a relatively small investment. Additionally, joining this community means being part of a group that values participation and collaboration, which aligns with my own passion for volunteering and mentoring others. My prior education and job experience as an Electrical Engineer have benefitted me as a software engineer and gives me an edge on my problem solving abilities for coding challenges. I am not easily over-whelmed and am able to break problems up into bite-sized chunks when peers are faced with complex issues.

I am pursuing a career that challenges me to continuously improve and is fulfilling in the work itself. I am always seeking opportunities to mentor others and to be in an environment where I can learn from others as well. I am extremely excited about the next chapter of my professional journey. I would love to chat and hear about what excites you!
